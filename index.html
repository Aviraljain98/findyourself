Stress

so what is stress

Stress is a reaction/response to any kind of change. It is the physical and emotional response to situations, which are perceived as novel, frightening, confusing, exciting or tiring. It not only gets precipitated by external demands, but can also be generated from internal aspects like our hopes, fears, expectations and beliefs.

what causes stress?

Different people react differently to stressful situations.

It depends on their perception and interpretation of events

Causes of Stress- External- 1) Daily events 2) Life events Internal 1. Beliefs and attitudes 2. Personality characteristics 3. Expectations 4. Past experience 5. Meaning in Life

When some worriers heard this, they laughed it out. Low score in Exams, critical illness, negative relationship with my family or friends... and you say it is all happening for the good? Spiritual wisdom relaxes us that whatever is happening in the present is right. Also, anything happening will strengthen you

Today, life is about completing tasks and making things happen faster and better. We feel the crunch of time, think that we are getting late, we are busy etc. Such situations create hurry and worry. They affect your mental, emotional, spiritual and physical health. If you feel you are getting late for college, for an assignment, for a meal, or getting ready give yourself the thought that I am always on time.

Every morning tell yourself “I am an actor on the world stage and everything I do here is my role to play.” An actor on a drama stage never identifies with his role. He knows the role is temporary and he has to return home after the role. Your role is temporary and not your real self. Your real self is full of qualities and powers. When you’re in a role where something goes awry, you may get stress.

You allow people or situations to control your thoughts, feelings and attitudes. Take the control in your hands. This means, don’t let thoughts be negative reactions to actions of others or events. People will be negative. Situations won’t be as you desire. But your thoughts should be positive, peaceful and powerful at all times. Stress will disappear when you begin to be your own masters at every moment.

Meditation

what is Meditation

The term Meditation comes from the Latin word “mederi” which means, to heal mentally, socially, spiritually and physically also. Meditation is deep and purposeful thought of eternal verities. It teaches us to have concentration, single track thinking and instant recognition of deviations from our intended focus. It helps us to distinguish between thought patterns and to select those which are positive, useful and lead us to our chosen goals.
